Connecting the building blocks to accessible care for families with disabilities

Web Design / SaaS / Accessibility

Project Overview

The cost of inefficiency

The problem

Service plans are required to help 8 million people in the United States with developmental disabilities live their best life. For each plan, an interdisciplinary team of physicians, psychologists, specialists, consultants, nurses, care managers or specific provider staff, and social workers may be required, and the meetings may span 30-minutes to 2 hours in duration, and may happen once to six times per year.

Though service plans are extensively coordinated to create a comprehensive roadmap for an individual’s livelihood and care, within the current healthcare system, service plans are often redrafted based on domain, settings, or providers, which creates double work for families, and the loss of time & money for other stakeholders. While parents will do anything for their child, it is an incredibly frustrating, lonely, and discouraging path.

The solution

The organization has created a SaaS product to connect the developmentally disabled with quality care that better-support families from emotional, time, and financial loss.

Our platform has 3 features, each one designed to overcome a major barrier to effective care for the disabled community.
